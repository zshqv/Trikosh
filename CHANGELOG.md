# Changelog

## [v0.1.0] — 2025-06-04

### Added
- 102 companies across 5 sectors with 5 years of standardized financial data
- 17 pre-computed financial ratios including P/E (from yFinance trailingPE) and EV/EBITDA (from Enterprise Value)
- YoY growth rates and 5-year revenue CAGR for all companies
- GAAP/IFRS reporting standard badge per company
- 7-step IB analyst research framework with sector-specific guidance
- Excel equity research template with 9 sheets and 254 formulas
- Peer comparison tool across sectors
- Clerk authentication
- Glossary with key financial terms

### Infrastructure
- Next.js 14 frontend deployed on Vercel
- Neon.tech PostgreSQL serverless database
- Python yFinance data pipeline
